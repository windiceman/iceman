NBA stands for National Basketball Association. The NBA been around for 70 years. There are 30 teams in the NBA. The Boston Celtics have the most championships in the NBA with 17. NBA have a 82 game regular season, and 4 rounds of the playoffs with a Best of 7. Also there are two conferences in the NBA; Eastern and Western. Some of the stars of the game are LeBron James, Paul George, Klay Thompson, James Harden, Kyrie Irving, Lamarcus Aldridge, Kyle Lowry, and more. Finally, the NBA is expanding globally to different countries.
