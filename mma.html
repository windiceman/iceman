UFC stands for Ultimate Fighting Championship. UFC currently growing as a sport across the world. UFC have so many fighters from different countries. UFC was founded on November 1993. UFC has quietly outperform and gaining more popularity than boxing. UFC uses 9 different weight classes which; strawweight, flyweight, bantamweight, featherweight, lightweight, welterweight, middleweight, light heavyweight, and heavyweight. UFC is a physical sport which the purpose is to knockout, submit your opponent, or win by decision. UFC brightest stars are Jon Jones, Demetrious Johnson, Dominick Cruz, Conor McGregor, and Robbie Lawler. Finally, UFC keeps growing every year and more people are watching it globally.
