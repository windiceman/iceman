NFL stand for National Football League. The NFL was founded in August 20, 1920. The NFL been around for 95 years. The current commissioner of the NFL is Rodger Goodell. The Green Bay Packers got the most titles with 13. The NFL is known as a physical sport with risk of brain issues, and concussion. People are scare to have their kids play football because of the physicality. There are 32 teams in the league with 16 teams in each conference. Six teams are selected from each conference. The playoff format is one game elimination. NFL has many stars with Tom Brady, Aaron Rodgers, Odell Beckham, Eli Manning, Adrian Peterson, Von Miller, Antonio Brown, and etc. At last, the Superbowl is most watched event in the month of February. Also the NFL is started to have their games in London, and China.
